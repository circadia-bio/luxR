# luxR: Light Exposure and Photic Regularity Analysis for Chronobiology Research

Tools for processing ambient light exposure data collected from
wrist-worn actigraphy devices (Axivity AX3/AX6 and ActTrust) in the
context of circadian and chronobiology research: photic exposure metrics
(mean/median illuminance, time above threshold, cumulative photic
history, melanopic EDI approximation) and light timing/regularity
metrics (light onset/offset detection, mean light timing, Light
Regularity Index).
